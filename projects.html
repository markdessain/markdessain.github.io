  • PlayHear is A collabrative playlist for any Facebook Page. (Added September 2013)
    - Download the following android app to add tracks to any Facebook page you are near.
    - As an admin of a facebook page you can view and play all the tracks that have been added to your playlist. As tracks are played they are removed off the list and all connected devices are kept in sync.

    APIs used: Facebook, Parse, Pusher and Deezer.

  • Eureka Languages Challenge. (Added October 2013)
    You'll hear a foreign word or phrase: Click on the phrase that you think is being said. The quicker you identify the phrase the more points you score but you ony get one shot. At the end we'll show you how much you understood of each of the languages that you heard.
    The harder you listen, the more you'll understand. Are you ready?
